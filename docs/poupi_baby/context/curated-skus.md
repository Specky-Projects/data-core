# Curated SKUs

Poupi Baby starts with a manually curated catalog of 100 to 300 SKUs.

## Why curated

Parents need trust more than breadth. A small catalog gives the platform enough control to maintain accurate matching, useful history and reliable alerts.

## Initial categories

- formulas
- fraldas
- lencos

## Selection criteria

- high purchase recurrence
- available in at least one initial store
- stable product identity across stores
- clear brand, size and variant
- useful price history for families

## Data rules

- each canonical product groups offers from supported stores
- each offer must point to a product URL, not a search page
- `external_id` must be stable per store
- category should use one of the initial category labels
- products outside the baby/farmacia vertical should not be added in the MVP

## Import approach

The first catalog can be maintained as a curated seed or admin import. Automated discovery should wait until the matching rules and historical data are reliable.
